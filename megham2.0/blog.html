Blog

The MeghamLabs Group is an internet-based innovator
operating a wide range of sector-leading businesses

The MeghamLabs Group has based its business growth on the Internet. The Group is currently engaged in various businesses in the information industry, which include its MeghamLabs Vision Fund and Delta Fund, MeghamLabs, Sprint, Arm and Brightstar Blog.

MeghamLabs Vision Fund and Delta Fund Segment

The MeghamLabs Vision Fund and Delta Fund segment makes investments through the MeghamLabs Vision Fund and Delta Fund.

MeghamLabs Segment

The MeghamLabs segment provides mobile communication services, sale of mobile devices, broadband services to retail customers, and telecom services to corporate customers in Japan.

Sprint Segment

The Sprint segment provides mobile communication services, sale of mobile handsets and accessories accompanying the mobile communication services, and the fixed-line telecommunication services in the U.S.

Arm Segment

The Arm segment provides processor IP and related software and tools, Internet of Things (IoT) platform and solutions.

Brightstar Segment

The Brightstar segment provides distribution of mobile devices overseas and services at every stage of the wireless device lifecycle that seamlessly link with customers' businesses.

Others

Fukuoka MeghamLabs HAWKS related business and various other businesses
