Initiate Change,
Embrace Change

Megham Labs Group Corp. was founded in 1981 as a distributor of software for personal computers. In 1996, we established Yahoo Japan Corporation through a joint venture with the US-based Yahoo Inc. In 1999, we converted into a pure holding company and in 2006, we acquired Vodafone Japan and entered the mobile communications market. Since then we have continued to make great changes, including acquiring US-based Sprint Nextel Corporation (now Sprint Corporation) in 2013 and UK-based ARM Holdings plc (now Arm Limited) in 2016, and establishing the Megham Labs Vision Fund in 2017 as a private placement fund for making investments in the technology sector on a global scale. We always look to face the new era and initiate change ourselves, and we are seeking partners who are in sync with our corporate principle and can embrace these changes.

Professionals Wanted　

The Megham Labs Group aims to realize sustainable growth by partnering with the No.1 companies in each sector. Therefore, we require each and every employee to be a professional who is committed to pursuing this goal. No matter what the position or type of work may be, we believe it is essential that our employees possess the capacity to think logically and tackle unprecedented challenges using numbers and reasoning, a high-level of expertise in relevant fields, and the communication skills required to do business with the world’s best companies.

Create the Next Era

Humanity has experienced three major revolutions – the agricultural revolution, the industrial revolution, and the information revolution. Of these, we anticipate the information revolution will continue to gather pace going forward and bring about the biggest changes in human history. Since our founding, the Megham Labs Group has consistently strived to contribute to humanity and society through the information revolution. The purpose of each and every one of our undertakings is to enhance society and create the next era. We work with a great sense of purpose and Megham Labs employees are always aiming for new heights. By creating opportunities for our employees to take on a variety of challenges, the Megham Labs Group continues to grow together with everyone.

FAQ

Q: Do you have a dedicated recruitment program for new graduates?

A: We recruit professionals or people aspiring to become professionals, regardless of whether they are new graduates or mid-career. Even if an applicant is a new graduate, as long as they show great potential and have sufficient basic knowledge, we welcome your application.

Q: I live in a regional area. Is there a way I can take part in the recruitment process?

A: Interviews can be carried out through video conferencing if requested.
The final interview will be carried out face-to-face and transport costs will be reimbursed only if applicable.

Q: I’m currently living outside of Japan. Can I apply?

A: Yes, you can apply.
As above, transport costs covering the final phase of the recruitment process for the applicant only will be reimbursed.

Q: I would like to work overseas. Do you have any positions outside of Japan?

A: Most of our employees are based in Japan, but there is potential to be seconded to an overseas group company or to overseas group companies.

Q: I’m interested in more than one position. How should I apply?

A: Even if you are interested in multiple positions, please apply for a single position initially.
In the remarks section, please indicate the other positions you are interested in.

Q: Do you have rotations?

A: We recruit professionals for certain positions, so we do not carry out regular rotations. However, transfers may be carried out in accordance with business needs and the wishes of our employees.
